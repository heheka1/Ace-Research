Mission Statement

For many years asexual history has been erased and ignored. One aim of this database is to combat asexual erasure and educate others about asexual history. This is done by collecting various historical articles, books, magazines, newspapers, and transcripts that address asexuality.

This database will also analyze the way in which the discussion of asexuality has evolved throughout the years.

What Even Is Asexuality?

Asexuality describes an indivdual that experiences little to no sexual attraction. Asexuality is a spectrum and can appear in various different ways. There is no "right" way to be asexual.

There are also sub-identities within the orientation that indivduals may identify with such as:

(this is not an exhaustive list)

  • Demisexual
  • Gray Asexual
  • Aegosexuality
